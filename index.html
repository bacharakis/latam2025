📅 Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Cotopaxi National Park – Hike & Bike an Active Volcano 🌋

Start in Quito and drive to Cotopaxi. Hike to the José Rivas Refuge (4,864m), trek to the glacier, then mountain bike down to Limpiopungo Lagoon.

Day 2-3: Baños – Extreme Sports & Waterfalls 🚀💦

Day 2: Bike the "Ruta de las Cascadas", canyoning, and visit the Swing at the End of the World. Relax at the hot springs.

Day 3: Whitewater rafting, bungee jumping, and spa time at Luna Volcán.

Day 4-5: Galápagos – Snorkeling with Sharks & Wildlife 🦈🐢

Day 4: Visit Tortuga Bay, swim in Las Grietas, and see giant tortoises at the Charles Darwin Research Station.

Day 5: Snorkel at Los Túneles (Isabela) or Kicker Rock (San Cristóbal) with sharks, sea lions, and turtles.

Day 6-9: Kitesurfing in Santa Marianita! 🏄‍♂️🌊

Take lessons in one of Ecuador’s top kitesurfing spots with strong winds and warm water.

💡 Travel Tips

  • 🏨 Best places to stay: Selina Baños, Hotel Cucuve (Galápagos), Ocean Freaks (Santa Marianita).
  • 🍽️ Must-try food: Ceviche, empanadas de viento, and Ecuadorian seafood.
  • 🚗 Best transport: Flights for long distances, buses for budget-friendly travel.
